Our Values

Our core values are designed to guide our business operations and provide a framework with which to guide and evaluate our most valuable asset – our global employees. These are the four key values that inspire the work conducted at Ardent Credit Services.

  • Compassion

    We operate with humility, caring, and respect, and strive to provide exceptional customer service during difficult circumstances.

  • Compliance

    We make investments and work to continually improve our compliance processes and systems, implementing proactive solutions to protect our clients and their brand reputation.

  • Commitment

    We keep our promises, we live up to our expectations, and we give our best while supporting our customers and clients.

  • Innovation

    We foster a work environment where creative thinking is encouraged to improve customer service, streamline business operations, improve quality, and bring value to our clients.
